RULES AND REGULATIONS

NO CLEATS OR STUDS PERMITTED. DRY AND CLEAN RUNNING SHOES OR INDOOR SOCCER SHOES ARE ONLY PERMITTED ON THE TURF.

ICE SKATES AND DRY AND CLEAN RUNNING SHOES ARE ONLY PERMITTED ON THE SYNTHETIC ICE.

NO WET SHOES ALLOWED ANYWHERE IN THE FACILITY (SNOW/RAIN/MUD).

OUTDOOR SHOES MUST BE REMOVED UPON ENTRY, WE ARE A SOCK ONLY FACILITY.

HELMETS MUST BE WORN ON THE SYNTHETIC ICE. SHIN PADS ARE RECOMMENDED ON THE TURF.

NO GUM, FOOD OR DRINKS ON THE TURF OR SYNTHETIC ICE.

NO NUTS OR NUT PRODUCTS IN THE FACILITY AS WE ARE A NUT AWARE SPACE.

NO SPARKLERS OR FOUNTAIN CANDLES.

NO GUM, POPCORN, SMALL CANDY OR CONFETTI PERMITTED.

NO FIGHTING AND NO BULLYING.

NO SMOKING, VAPING OR DRUG USE.

ALL RULES MUST BE FOLLOWED OR YOU WILL BE REMOVED AND/OR BANNED FROM THE FACILITY.

PARTY HOST WILL HAVE 10-15 MINUTES PRIOR TO THE EVENT FOR SET UP/ENTRY TIME.

AT THE EVENT'S END TIME YOU MUST BE PACKED UP AND READY TO EXIT THE FACILITY. PLEASE BE RESPECTFUL TOWARDS THE NEXT PARTY COMING IN.

A $300 DEPOSIT IS REQUIRED TO BOOK AN EVENT. ALL DEPOSITS ARE NON-REFUNDABLE.

PAYMENT AND WAIVER MUST BE COMPLETED PRIOR TO ANY RENTAL STARTING.

THE FACILITY MUST BE LEFT IN THE SAME CONDITION IT WAS UPON ENTRY. IF ANY DAMAGES OR EXCESSIVE CLEANING IS NECESSARY THE $250 DAMAGE DEPOSIT WILL BE RETAINED TO COVER OR PARTIALLY COVER THE CLEANING AND/OR DAMAGES.

NO BALL/PUCK PLAY ANYWHERE OTHER THAN THE TURF OR SYNTHETIC ICE.

CHILDREN 3 AND UNDER ARE FREE.

LIGHTS OUT SPORTS IS NOT RESPONSIBLE FOR ANY LOST, DAMAGED OR STOLEN ITEMS.

LIGHTS OUT SPORTS WILL NOT BE HELD RESPONSIBLE FOR ANY INJURIES, DEATH, ACCIDENTS, ACTS OF GOD OR THE LIKE WHILE USING THE FACILITY.
