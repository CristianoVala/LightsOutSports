About Us

Host your next event or training at LIGHTS OUT SPORTS, the IT spot in Alliston for Glow in the Dark Entertainment.

Two separate spaces to book your event, one has synthetic ice and the other has artifical turf. A variety of sports can be played in these areas: soccer, lacross, flag football, ultimate frisbee, dodgeball, field hockey, lawn bowling, pickleball, badminton, hockey, skating, curling.

Glow in the dark is fun for celebrations and events such as birthday parties, corporate events, team building, community events for a fun time out together. The glow in the dark can also be turned off for more formal training and developmental sessions.
