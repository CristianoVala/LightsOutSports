TRAINING

Glow in the dark can be turned off and regular white lights will be on for more formal training and developmental sessions.

The artificial turf can be used for a variety of training including soccer, field hockey, lacross, lawn bowling, flag football, ultimate frisbee, dodgeball, pickleball, or badminton. The synthetic ice can be used for hockey, skating, or curling.

$75 per hour time slot

Can arrive 5 mins early for set up and must be cleared out on time to be respectful to the next party coming in.

A maximum of 30 guests per reservation.

-Private field and private washroom

-Tables and chairs

-Bluetooth speaker, free Wifi

-No food or drinks ARE permitted.

-Host is responsible for set up and to clean up after their guests.
