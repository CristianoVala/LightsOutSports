CAMPS

School Age fun is available at Lights Out Sports. We are available for March Break, Summer Camp, PA Days and Winter Break.

Operating hours are 8am to 5pm

Daily rate of $65/day or $300/week

10% sibling discount

Ages 4 to 12

A maximum of 30 children per group, children will be divided by age groups, a group with younger and a group with older children.

You can choose if your child participates in both hockey and soccer or hockey only or soccer only.

Fun games and engaging activities throughout the day including sports but also science, crafts, and outdoor play time.

At Lights Out Sports, our staff are experienced professionals who are passionate about working with children. Every team member has a valid police background check and is fully trained in CPR and first aid, ensuring a safe and supportive environment for all participants.

Children will need to bring:

-Refillable water bottle

-Lunch and snacks, every Friday is pizza day (cheese or pepperoni). All food must be nut free.

-Outdoor shoes and clean and dry indoor running shoes

-If participating in hockey, will need helmet and stick, gloves are recommended

-If participating in soccer, will need their own shin pads

BEFORE & AFTER SCHOOL SPORTS CLUB

A before and after school sports program for children between the ages of 4 and 12 years old is designed to provide physical activity, skill development, and supervised recreation outside of regular school hours. Lights Out Sports promotes a healthy lifestyle through daily activity. This program helps children develop teamwork, coordination and social skills while providing a safe and engaging environment for before and after school. It also promotes better focus and behaviour during school and exposure to new sports and activities. Every session offers light stretching, sports drills and short games along with a snack. Our staff are CPR trained and first aid certified with backgrounds in youth sports and offer an inclusive approach for any skill level. The rotating curriculum includes a variety of sports and activities to keep children engaged and learning.

Before School - 7:00am to school bus stop pick up at Lights Out Sports or private transportation drop off at school

After School - school bus drop off at Lights Out Sports or private transportation pick up at the school until 5:30pm

School bus transportation for Ernest Cumberland Public School

Private transportation for Tottenham Public School and St James Elementary School

School bus transportation daily rate is $40

Private transportation daily rate is $45
