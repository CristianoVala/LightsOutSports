BIRTHDAY PARTY PACKAGE

$675 - 2.5 hours time slot includes food

$625 - 2.5 hours time slot excludes food

Can arrive 15 mins early for set up and must be cleared out on time to be respectful to the next party coming in.

This is a glow in the dark event, white lights will remain off during the party.

Up to 15 Children included in the package ($15 for each additional child up to a maximum of 22)

Birthday child and siblings are included in the 15. Guests aged 4 to 17 are counted as part of the guests, regardless of participation. They will be counted as part of the 15. Children 3 years of age and under are excluded.

Adults are not counted. A maximum of 30 adults per reservation in addition to the 15 children for a total of 45 guests.

This package includes:

-Private party room, private field and private washroom

-Decorative birthday sign, balloon arch and centerpieces

-Tables and chairs

-Coat rack and outdoor shoe area. (We are a sock facility so no outdoor shoes are permitted except for indoor shoes on the turf and skates on the ice.)

-Glow-in-the-dark balls/pucks and pinnies

-Referee for one hour

-Foosball or air hockey table

-Jumbo Connect Four, Jumbo Jenga

-Gaming console with 2 controllers

-Bluetooth speaker, free Wifi

-2 Party size (cheese or pepperoni) pizzas

-Case of 24 water bottles

-Coffee machine, tea and coffee pods

-Large bowls, disposable plates, and disposable utensils

-Outside food and drinks ARE permitted. No nut or nut products. Cake is not provided.

-Host is responsible for set up and to clean up after their guests.
